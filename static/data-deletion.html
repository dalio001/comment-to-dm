Data Deletion Instructions

Pure Living Zone — Comment Assistant. Last updated: 21 June 2026.

You have the right to request deletion of any data the Pure Living Zone Comment Assistant holds about you. We store very little — typically only the platform-scoped ID assigned to our Page and a record that your comment was handled.

How to request deletion

  1. Email ouaja.mohamedali@gmail.com with the subject line “Data Deletion Request”.
  2. Include the Facebook or Instagram name/handle you used to comment, and the approximate date.
  3. We will delete all associated records within 30 days and confirm by reply.

Immediate self-service

You can also delete the message thread in Messenger or Instagram and block the Page at any time, which stops all further messages immediately.

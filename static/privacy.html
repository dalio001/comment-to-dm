Privacy Policy

Pure Living Zone — Comment Assistant. Last updated: 21 June 2026.

This Privacy Policy explains how the Pure Living Zone Comment Assistant ("the App", "we") handles information when it responds to comments on our Facebook Page and Instagram account. The App is operated by Pure Living Zone.

1. Information we process

When you comment on one of our posts that runs an automated campaign, or when you message our Page, we process:

  • Your public comment text and comment ID;
  • Your Facebook/Instagram display name and the platform-scoped user ID (PSID/IGSID) assigned to our Page;
  • The content of messages you send to our Page in reply, and messages we send to you.

We do not collect your email, phone number, password, payment details, or friends list.

2. How we use it

  • To reply publicly to your comment;
  • To send you the resource (e.g. a guide link) you requested via direct message;
  • To send a single follow-up message once you reply, and to avoid messaging you twice for the same comment.

3. Legal basis & retention

We process this data to respond to your explicit request (your comment/keyword). We retain the minimum needed — your platform-scoped ID and a record that a comment was handled — for as long as the related campaign is active, and delete it on request (see below).

4. Sharing

We do not sell or share your data with third parties for advertising. Data is processed only through Meta's official Graph API and our own hosting provider (Render) and database (Neon), solely to operate this messaging feature.

5. Your rights & data deletion

You can request access to or deletion of your data at any time. See our Data Deletion Instructions, or email ouaja.mohamedali@gmail.com. You can also remove the conversation in Messenger/Instagram and block the Page to stop all messages.
